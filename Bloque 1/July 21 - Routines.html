Lesson 2

The Routine of Others (He/She) 👑

When we talk about He, She, or It, the verb changes. Get to know ALL the famous "S" rules!

1. General Rule (+s)

Add 's' to most verbs.

wake wakes
read reads

2. Ends in: o, x, ss, ch, sh (+es)

Add 'es' so we can pronounce it.

go goes
watch watches

3. Consonant + Y (+ies)

Drop the 'y' and add 'ies'.

study studies
cry cries

4. Irregular Exception

The verb "Have" changes completely.

have has
Ex: He has breakfast.

Lesson 4

Time and Places (In/On/At) 📍

These magic words help us explain WHERE and WHEN things happen.

IN

Enclosed spaces & Parts of the day

in the kitchen, in my bedroom, in the morning.

ON

Surfaces & Days of the week

on the bus, on the street, on Monday.

AT

Specific points & Exact times

at home, at work, at 8:00 AM, at night.

Lesson 5

Making Questions (Do / Does) ❓

To ask a question in the Present Simple, we need auxiliary verbs: Do or Does. They always go at the beginning!

Do / Does + Subject + Verb (Base) ?

With "Do" (I, You, We, They)

👉 Do you like music? 🎧

No 'S' here!

With "Does" (He, She, It)

👉 Does she cook? 🍳

The 'S' disappears from the main verb because "Does" already has it.
